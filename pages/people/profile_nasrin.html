Biographical Sketch
Nasrin is currently a graduate research assistant with the VIVA group. Nasrin completed her bachelors in Electrical Engineering in 2005 from K.N. Toosi University of Technology, Iran and her MS in Physics from University of Wyoming in 2015.
Research Interests
Nasrin's research interest lies in the field of video analysis and machine learning. During her MS, Nasrin was working on phonon-electron simulation in the photosynthesis process. She has also done works in optimization, mainly on multi-agent multi-target task assignment problem.
